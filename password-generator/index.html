Frequently asked questions

What makes a password truly strong? ↓
A strong password combines uppercase & lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols, while being at least 12 characters long. Our generator ensures maximum entropy by randomly selecting from all chosen character sets.
Are the passwords stored anywhere? ↓
No. Everything happens inside your browser using JavaScript. No passwords are saved, logged, or transmitted. Your data stays 100% private.
How often should I update my passwords? ↓
Security experts recommend changing passwords every 90 days, or immediately after any suspected breach. Use unique passwords for every account — this generator makes that easy.
